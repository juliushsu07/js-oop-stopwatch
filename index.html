This is an OOP STOPWATCH WEB APP EXCERCISE!!!!

Step 1: Right-click anywhere inside the page and click on inspect!

Step 2: Go to the Console tab!

Step 3: Enter in console: const sw = new Stopwatch() to initialze.

  • To start to the watch, please enter sw.start() in the console!
  • To stop to the watch, please enter sw.stop() in the console!
  • To get the duration, please enter sw.duration in the console!
  • To reset to the watch, please enter sw.reset() in the console!
